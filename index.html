Cognitive Trap Repository

Community tools for detecting AI agents in online surveys

Cognitive traps are visual-perceptual tasks that exploit architectural constraints in vision-language models. Humans pass them easily; AI agents fail systematically. Each trap is validated against several models from Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google across multiple generations. Browse the collection, click any trap to see full details and download the stimulus.
